# Applicant Project Description (As Submitted)

---

## Project Title

**Mid-State Freight Rail Safety & Capacity Improvement Project**

---

## Program Type

**CRISI (Illustrative)**

---

## Project Scope

The proposed project upgrades **12 at-grade rail crossings** and modernizes signal systems along a **40-mile freight rail corridor** serving regional agricultural and industrial shippers.

Improvements include:

- Installation of updated warning systems  
- Crossing surface rehabilitation  
- Signal interconnectivity upgrades  
- Corridor reliability enhancements  

---

## Capital Cost (Year 0 Dollars)

**$82,000,000**

---

## Annual Operations & Maintenance Cost

**$1,500,000**

---

## Project Evaluation Period

**20 years**

---

## Primary Benefit Categories Identified by Applicant

- Reduction in rail-highway grade crossing incidents  
- Travel time savings due to improved train speeds and reduced delay  
- Reduced vehicle operating costs  
- Emissions reductions
